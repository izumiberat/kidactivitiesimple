Privacy Policy for PlayIdeas

Last updated: 30/06/2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

PlayIdeas does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. Our app operates entirely offline after initial download.

2. Local Storage

The app stores your favorite activities locally on your device using secure storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or third parties.

3. No Third-Party Services

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services in PlayIdeas.

4. Children's Privacy

While PlayIdeas provides activity ideas for children, the app itself does not collect any information from children or require any personal details.
